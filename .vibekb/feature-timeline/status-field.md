## Why not just notes?

Operators needed a glanceable signal: exploring vs pursuing vs done.

## Rejected

- Kanban board (too much UI for v1)
- Auto-archive by date (hid active thinking)
- More than three states (premature taxonomy)

## Depends on

Ideas CRUD, manual migrations, single-user assumption.
